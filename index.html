Why us after a breakdown?

City To Coast Mobile Mechanical aims to satisfy our customers. This means getting you on the road again FAST!

Breaking down on the M1 can be a stressful experience. Tow trucks can charge upwards of a hundred dollars to simply move your broken down car. It’s much more cost-effective to call us at City To Coast Mobile Mechanical. We are a workshop on wheels with many different fluids, mobile automotive testing equipment, jacks and gaskets ready to get your vehicle out on the road again FAST. We provide breakdown assistance with the most cost effective solutions.

Flat tyre?

Experts in all things automotive, City To Coast Mobile Mechanical has you covered and will have you on the road again hassle free.

Flat tyres may require temporary puncture repairs or other adjustments.

You can have new tyres fitted by a mobile tyre fitter mermaid beach (wheels taken with your vehicle left secure at your home), or arrange to have the vehicle taken to a tyre shop.

Do you do Diesel repairs?

Yes. City To Coast Mobile Mechanical have extensive training in all types of cars, 4wd and light trucks.

With a popular trend in turbo diesel common rail engines in utes, 4×4 and trucks around the Gold Coast region, mobile diesel repairs & servicing is carried out on a regular basis.

We can assist you with your diesel breakdown, service or repair.

Flat Battery Breakdown?

City To Coast Mobile Mechanical can provide you with new Century Batteries fitted on site or just a simple jump start/charging battery system check to get you on the road again.

Vehicles are not the same and neither are vehicle batteries so matching the correct battery is critical for the life of the battery and the vehicle’s electrical systems. It is important to have the correct battery fitted and use the correct method, tooling, or in a lot of late model vehicles, the correct computer coding of the battery to the vehicle’s electrical system.

Call/Text now 0412-962-428 if your car is showing the following symptoms:

  • Nil start
  • Engine ‘winding over’ slowly
  • Electrical systems failing
  • Engine check/warning light
  • Driving problems

Smoke/steam coming from engine bay?

Probably one of the most daunting breakdown situations is assuming that your engine has ‘blown up’ when smoke is coming from the engine bay, exhaust pipe, or bonnet.

City To Coast Mobile Mechanical has plenty of experience with this issue and can usually have the vehicle repaired with the replacement of a broken coolant hose, oil fitting or bypass on a system. This can be done on the side of the road to have you motoring again FAST.

Unfortunately not all fixes are able to be performed without a tow to a workshop. City To Coast Mobile Mechanical use the latest diagnostic equipment to quickly uncover the issue. Your car can be taken to a fully licensed repair location for gasket replacement or even a replacement engine.
